This is KPMG, and this is our Story

"The Nutcracker and the Mouse King" is a story written in 1816 by German author E. T. A. Hoffmann. In the famous fairytale brothers Clara and Fritz have a very special godfather who makes inventions out of clockwork and his Christmas presents are always amazing!

The Nutcracker story

This year he gives them something rather small and simple - a nutcracker-doll in the form of a soldier. It's rather ugly, and soon it is broken, but Clara loves it all the same. And then it comes to life and proves himself to be a true hero. The Nutcracker after defeating the evil Mouse King in battle, whisks her away to a magical kingdom populated by dolls.

To us the Nutcracker is more than a beloved Christmas story. It is our source of inspiration for our Christmas wishes this year.

The Nutcracker’s story as an iconic hero who fights and protects Clara, symbolizes and illustrates care for those in need.

In KPMG we do care for people. Care is very important for us and a crucial part of our DNA.

We don’t only care about companies we work with, but we also cherish the bond they have with their people and the wider society and we are by their side helping them respond to changes in the global economy.

Regardless of our individual role in KPMG, we make a positive impact in society through the work we do. By staying focused on what really matters, and acting as stewards for the next generation, we aim to inspire confidence and empower change.
